INTRODUCTION TO CIVIL ENGINEERING BESCK104A

VTU NOTES

Subject code BESCK104A/204
Semester 1st / 2nd

MODULE - 1

Civil Engineering Disciplines and Building Science
Introduction to Civil Engineering: Surveying, Structural Engineering, Geotechnical Engineering, Hydraulics and water Resources, Transportation Engineering, Environmental Engineering, Construction planning and project management.

Basic Materials of Construction: Bricks, Cement & mortars, Plain, Reinforced &Pre-stressed Concrete, Structural steel, Construction Chemicals.

Structural elements of a building: foundation, plinth, lintel, chejja, Masonry wall, column, beam, slab and staircase

MODULE - 2

Surveying and Geomatics:
Introduction to surveying, Principles of surveying, Linear and angular measurements, leveling, contouring, and modern surveying instruments like Total Station and GPS.

Water Resources Engineering: Principles of hydrology, Water cycle, Measurement of precipitation, Runoff, and introduction to dams and reservoirs.

MODULE - 3

Structural Engineering:
Introduction to various types of structures, concepts of load and stress, and an overview of design principles for beams, columns, and trusses.

Geotechnical Engineering: Introduction to soil mechanics, Types of soil, Soil properties, and basic concepts of foundation engineering.

MODULE - 4

Transportation Engineering:
Overview of different modes of transportation, Introduction to highway engineering, geometric design of roads, and basic concepts of traffic engineering.

Environmental Engineering: Concepts of water supply engineering, wastewater treatment, and solid waste management.

MODULE - 5

Construction Management:
Introduction to construction planning, project management, project life cycle, and an overview of construction materials, equipment, and safety.
